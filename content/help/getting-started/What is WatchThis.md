WatchThis helps you find what to watch, organize it into lists, and keep your progress in sync across devices (and optionally with collaborators).

## What You Can Do

### Discover movies and TV shows

- Browse **Trending Today** or search by title.
- Filter by **Content Type**, **Genre**, **Year**, and **Sort By**.
- Open a title to see details, cast, and streaming providers (based on your region and selected services).

Learn more in [Find Movies and TV Shows](/help/discover/Find%20Movies%20and%20TV%20Shows) and [Where to Watch](/help/discover/Where%20to%20Watch).

### Organize with lists

- Create lists for anything (watch later, comfort shows, “with friends”, etc.).
- Keep lists **Private** or make them **Public**.
- Add content from the details view (no copy/paste required).
- See **Recommended** suggestions based on what’s already in a list.

Start here: [Create and Manage Lists](/help/lists/Create%20and%20Manage%20Lists) and [Add and Remove Items from a List](/help/lists/Add%20and%20Remove%20Items%20from%20a%20List).

### Track what you’ve watched

WatchThis stores your watch status separately from your lists, so you can keep progress even as you reorganize.

- Movies: **Planning** or **Completed**
- TV: **Planning**, **Watching**, **Paused**, **Completed**, or **Dropped**

Read: [Watch Status Explained](/help/tracking/Watch%20Status%20Explained).

### Track TV by episode (and schedule your week)

For TV shows, you can:

- Mark individual episodes watched.
- Schedule shows for specific days of the week.
- Use the “next episode” prompt on scheduled days to stay consistent.

Read: [Track TV Episodes](/help/tracking/Track%20TV%20Episodes), [Weekly TV Schedule](/help/tracking/Weekly%20TV%20Schedule), and [Mark the Next Episode Watched](/help/tracking/Mark%20the%20Next%20Episode%20Watched).

### Collaborate with friends (optional)

Share a list with others and choose whether they can edit it or view it.

If you enable syncing on a shared list, WatchThis can also sync watch status and TV schedules across collaborators for content in that list.

Read: [Share a List with Collaborators](/help/collaboration/Share%20a%20List%20with%20Collaborators) and [How Sync Works in Shared Lists](/help/collaboration/How%20Sync%20Works%20in%20Shared%20Lists).
