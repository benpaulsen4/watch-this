WatchThis can show streaming providers for movies and TV shows, but it needs your **Country/Region** and (optionally) which providers you subscribe to. This powers the **Streaming** section in the content details view.

## Set Your Country/Region

1. Go to **Profile** (`/profile`).
2. Open the **Streaming** tab (or use `/profile#streaming`).
3. Under **Country/Region**, choose your country.

WatchThis uses your country to pull provider availability for that region.

## Select Your Providers (Optional)

Selecting providers helps WatchThis highlight the services you care about.

1. In the **Providers** section, use **Search providers...** to find a service.
2. Click providers to select or deselect them.
3. Use **Previous** / **Next** to browse more providers if needed.
4. Click **Save Preferences**.

## Verify It Worked

1. Open any movie or TV show details.
2. In **Overview**, find the **Streaming** section.
3. Confirm providers appear for your country, and your selected providers are emphasized.

If you don’t see results, see [Region Provider Results Look Wrong](/help/faq/Region%20Provider%20Results%20Look%20Wrong) and [Where to Watch](/help/discover/Where%20to%20Watch).
