Shared lists always share the list’s items. Syncing is an optional, separate feature that can also synchronize watch progress and TV scheduling across collaborators.

## Two Types of “Sharing”

### 1) Shared list items (always on)

When someone adds or removes a title in a shared list, the list contents are updated for everyone who has access to that list.

Permissions still apply:

- **Collaborators** can edit list items.
- **Viewers** are read-only.

Read: [Manage Collaborator Permissions](/help/collaboration/manage-collaborator-permissions).

### 2) Shared progress syncing (optional)

The list owner can enable:

**Sync watch status & TV schedules with collaborators**

When enabled, WatchThis syncs progress for titles that are in that specific list, including:

- Overall watch status (Planning/Watching/Completed, etc.)
- Episode watched/unwatched progress for TV
- Weekly schedule selections for TV

## What Sync Is Scoped To

Sync applies only to titles that are included in the shared list with syncing enabled.

- If a title isn’t in the list, syncing won’t happen for that title.
- If you remove a title from the list, future progress changes won’t sync via that list.

## What to Expect in Practice

- Sync is designed for “watch together” lists where everyone wants the same progress.
- Updates are not instant messages; collaborators may need to refresh or revisit a page to see changes reflected in their UI.
