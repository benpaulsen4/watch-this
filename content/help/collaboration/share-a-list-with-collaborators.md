Sharing a list lets you collaborate with friends or family. You control who can edit the list and whether watch progress should sync across collaborators.

## Before You Start

- You must be the **list owner** to add or remove collaborators.
- You’ll need the other person’s **username** in WatchThis.

## Invite a Collaborator

1. Open the list you want to share.
2. Click **Share**.
3. In **Add Collaborator**, enter the person’s username.
4. Choose a permission level:
   - **Collaborator** (can edit the list)
   - **Viewer** (read-only)
5. Click **Add**.

![Manage Collaborators Modal](/help/collaboration/manage-collaborators.png)

The collaborator will see the list in their lists view.

## Remove a Collaborator

1. Open the list → **Share**.
2. In the **Collaborators** section, click the trash icon next to a user.
3. Confirm removal.

## Optional: Sync Watch Status and Schedules

In **Settings**, the list owner can enable:

**Sync watch status & TV schedules with collaborators**

When enabled, watch status changes, episode progress, and TV schedules can synchronize across collaborators for titles in that list.

Read: [How Sync Works in Shared Lists](/help/collaboration/how-sync-works-in-shared-lists).
