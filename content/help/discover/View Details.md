When you click a movie or TV show in WatchThis, it opens a details view where you can explore information and take action (track, schedule, or add to lists).

## Open the Details View

1. Find a title in **Discover** or in a list.
2. Click the poster card to open details.

## What You’ll Find Inside

### Overview

The **Overview** tab shows:

- A plot summary (when available)
- Genres
- Streaming providers (based on your region and provider preferences)

To set streaming preferences, see [Set Your Region and Streaming Providers](/help/getting-started/Set%20Your%20Region%20and%20Streaming%20Providers).

### Cast

Open **Cast** to browse key cast members for the title.

### Lists

Use the **Lists** tab to add or remove the title from your lists without leaving the page.

Read: [Add and Remove Items from a List](/help/lists/Add%20and%20Remove%20Items%20from%20a%20List).

### Episodes (TV only)

For TV shows, the **Episodes** tab helps you track episode-by-episode progress.

Read: [Track TV Episodes](/help/tracking/Track%20TV%20Episodes).

### Schedule (TV only)

If you’ve set a watch status for a TV show (for example **Planning** or **Watching**), you’ll also see a **Schedule** tab. This lets you schedule the show on specific days of the week.

Read: [Weekly TV Schedule](/help/tracking/Weekly%20TV%20Schedule).
