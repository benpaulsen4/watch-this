Use **Discover** to find movies and TV shows, then open any title to add it to a list, track your status, or explore episodes.

## Open Discover

You can reach Discover from:

- The dashboard (**Discover**)
- A list page (**Add Content**)

## Search by Title

1. Go to **Discover**.
2. Type a title into the search bar.
3. Results update as you search.

When you’re searching, WatchThis focuses on matching titles and limits some filters (see [Browse by Genre and Filters](/help/discover/Browse%20by%20Genre%20and%20Filters)).

## Browse Trending Today

If the search box is empty, Discover shows **Trending Today** plus a browsable grid of content.

- Scroll to explore, then use **Load More** to fetch additional pages.
- If you don’t see anything, check that your filters are cleared (or try again later).
