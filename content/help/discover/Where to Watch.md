WatchThis can show which streaming providers carry a movie or TV show in your country/region.

Provider availability varies by region, so the same title may show different providers for different users.

## How “Where to Watch” Works

1. Set your **Country/Region** in Profile.
2. Open a title’s details view.
3. In **Overview**, find the **Streaming** section.

Your selected providers are highlighted so you can quickly spot services you subscribe to.

## Set Up Your Region and Providers

If the **Streaming** section asks you to configure settings, set them here:

- [Set Your Region and Streaming Providers](/help/getting-started/Set%20Your%20Region%20and%20Streaming%20Providers)

## What’s Included (and What Isn’t)

- WatchThis focuses on subscription and ad-supported options.
- Some regions may show no providers for a title even when it exists elsewhere.
- Provider catalogs can change over time.

## Troubleshooting

### “No streaming providers found”

Try:

1. Confirm your **Country/Region** is set.
2. Save your preferences again (even if unchanged).
3. Check a different title to confirm it’s not title-specific.

### Providers look wrong for my country

Double-check the country/region in Profile and see [Region Provider Results Look Wrong](/help/faq/Region%20Provider%20Results%20Look%20Wrong).
