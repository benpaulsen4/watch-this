Filters help you narrow down what you see in **Discover** when you’re browsing (or searching) for movies and TV shows.

## Open Filters

1. Go to **Discover** (`/search`).
2. Click **Filters** to open the filter panel.

[[Image: Discover page with the Filters button and the Filters panel expanded.]]

## Filter Options

### Content Type

Choose:

- **All** (movies and TV shows)
- **Movies**
- **TV Shows**

### Genre

Pick a single genre (or **All Genres**) to focus your browse results.

### Year

Use **Year** to focus on a release window.

Note: the Year filter works best when you’ve selected **Movies** or **TV Shows** (some search providers don’t support “year” for mixed results).

### Sort By

Choose how results are ordered:

- **Popularity**
- **Rating**
- **Release Date**
- **Title**

## Clear Filters

Click **Clear Filters** to reset everything back to the defaults.

## Tips

- If you want to browse by genre or popularity, clear the search box so you’re in browse mode.
- If you’re searching by title, focus on **Content Type** and (when available) **Year**.
