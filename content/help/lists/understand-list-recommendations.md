On some list pages, you’ll see a **Recommended** section. These suggestions are based on the content already in that list.

## What Recommendations Are Based On

WatchThis looks at patterns in the list, such as:

- Common genres
- Common keywords and themes
- Frequently appearing cast (especially for movies)

It then finds candidate titles and ranks them by similarity plus general quality signals (for example: rating, popularity, and recency).

## How Many Recommendations You’ll See

When possible, WatchThis shows a small set of suggestions (enough to explore without overwhelming the page).

## When Recommendations Refresh

Recommendations refresh when:

- You add or remove items from the list, or
- It’s been a while since the last refresh

This helps keep suggestions relevant without changing them constantly.

## Why You Might Not See Recommendations

Common reasons:

- The list is empty (there’s nothing to base recommendations on).
- The list doesn’t have enough signal yet (for example, a brand new list with one item).
- The list is archived (recommendations are hidden).

## Tips for Better Recommendations

- Add a few items that clearly match what you want (for example, 5–10 titles).
- Keep lists focused (a Movies Only or TV Shows Only list often yields clearer suggestions).
