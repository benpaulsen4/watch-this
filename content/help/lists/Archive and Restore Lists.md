Archiving is a way to hide lists you’re not using right now without deleting them. Archived lists appear in a separate view and can be restored at any time.

## Archive a List

1. Open the list you want to archive.
2. Click **Settings**.
3. Click **Archive List**.

When a list is archived:

- It appears under **Archived Lists**.
- Syncing is turned off for that list.
- Recommendations are hidden for that list.

## View Archived Lists

1. Go to **My Lists** (`/lists`).
2. Click **Archive** to open **Archived Lists**.

## Restore an Archived List

1. Open **Archived Lists**.
2. Open the archived list.
3. Click **Settings**.
4. Click **Unarchive List**.

## Delete vs Archive

- **Archive**: best when you might want the list later.
- **Delete**: permanently removes the list, its items, and collaborators.
