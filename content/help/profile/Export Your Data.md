You can export your WatchThis data for backup, migrating to another account, or personal analysis.

## What’s Included

Exports include:

- Your lists and list items
- Your movie and TV watch statuses
- Your TV episode watched/unwatched progress
- Your weekly TV schedule

## Export as JSON

JSON is the best format for re-importing back into WatchThis.

1. Go to **Profile** (`/profile`).
2. Open the **Data** tab.
3. Under **Export Data**, click **Export as JSON**.

## Export as CSV (Zip)

CSV exports download as a `.zip` containing multiple `.csv` files (for lists, list items, statuses, episodes, and schedules).

1. Go to **Profile** → **Data**.
2. Click **Export as CSV**.
