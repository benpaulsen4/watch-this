Your profile controls how you appear to collaborators and how WatchThis schedules TV prompts for your local day.

## Open Profile Settings

Go to **Profile** (`/profile`) and use the **Profile** tab.

## Change Your Username

1. Click **Change Username**.
2. Enter your new username.
3. Click **Save**.

Usernames must be:

- 3–50 characters
- Letters, numbers, hyphen (`-`), or underscore (`_`)

If the name is taken, you’ll be asked to choose a different one.

## Add or Change Your Profile Picture

WatchThis currently uses an image URL (it does not upload files).

1. Click **Add Picture** (or **Change Picture**).
2. Paste a direct `http(s)` image URL.
3. Click **Save**.

To remove your picture, clear the URL and save.

## Change Your Timezone

Your timezone affects schedule and “today” prompts for TV shows.

1. Click **Change Timezone**.
2. Choose your timezone from the list.
3. Click **Save**.
