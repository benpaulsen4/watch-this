WatchThis uses passkeys so you can sign in securely without passwords.

## What a Passkey Is

A passkey is a modern sign-in method stored by your device (or password manager). Instead of typing a password, you approve a sign-in with Face ID, Touch ID, Windows Hello, or your device lock screen.

## Why WatchThis Uses Passkeys

- **No passwords to forget**: you don’t need to reset or manage passwords.
- **Phishing resistant**: passkeys are tied to the real site you’re visiting, which helps prevent “lookalike” login scams.
- **Stronger by default**: you get strong authentication without needing special rules.
- **Better multi-device experience**: passkeys can sync across your devices through your OS/password manager.

## What If I Lose a Device?

If your passkeys sync (for example with iCloud Keychain or Google Password Manager), you can often sign in from another device automatically.

If they don’t sync, add another passkey device ahead of time:
