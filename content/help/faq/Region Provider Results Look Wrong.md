Streaming availability varies by country/region and by provider. If the **Streaming** section doesn’t match what you expect, it’s usually due to region settings or catalog differences.

## 1) Verify Your Country/Region

1. Go to **Profile** (`/profile`).
2. Open the **Streaming** tab.
3. Confirm **Country/Region** is correct.
4. Click **Save Preferences** (even if unchanged).

Read: [Set Your Region and Streaming Providers](/help/getting-started/Set%20Your%20Region%20and%20Streaming%20Providers).

## 2) Check What WatchThis Highlights

Selected providers are emphasized so you can spot your services quickly. If nothing is highlighted, you may not have any providers selected (that doesn’t prevent results, it just affects highlighting).

## 3) Remember: Catalogs Differ from App to App

Provider catalogs can differ based on:

- Your region
- Subscription plan and add-ons
- Title licensing changes over time

It’s normal for a title to show providers you don’t personally subscribe to.

## 4) “No Providers Found” for a Specific Title

Try a different title to rule out a title-specific gap. Some titles simply don’t have provider data for your region.

## 5) Expectations: What WatchThis Shows

WatchThis focuses on subscription and ad-supported availability in the details view. If you’re looking specifically for rent/buy options, you may need to check providers directly.
