WatchThis lets you quickly track your progress without opening every show's details page. The fastest way is to simply double-tap the show card.

## Quick Method: Double-Tap

You can mark the **next available episode** as watched from any list, search result, or your dashboard:

1.  Find the TV show card.
2.  **Double-tap** (or double-click) anywhere on the card.
3.  A green checkmark will appear over the poster, confirming the episode (e.g., "S1E1: Pilot") is marked as watched.

This automatically:

- Marks the next unwatched episode as "watched".
- Updates your show status (e.g., from "Planning" to "Watching").
- Syncs with your collaborators if enabled.

## Using the Weekly Schedule Prompt

If you use the **Weekly Schedule**, WatchThis also provides a dedicated prompt on your dashboard:

You’ll see an **upcoming** card in your activity feed on days when:

- A show is scheduled for today, and
- You haven’t already watched an episode of that show today

![Activity Feed Prompt](/help/tracking/upcoming-activity.png)

To use it:

1.  Find the scheduled show card in your activity feed.
2.  Click the **Episode Watched** button.

## If You’re "Caught Up"

If double-tapping doesn't seem to work or the button says you're caught up, it usually means:

- **No new episodes:** You’ve watched all available episodes.
- **Not aired yet:** The next episode hasn't been released.

In these cases, you can check the show’s full episode list manually.

Read: [Track TV Episodes](/help/tracking/track-tv-episodes).
