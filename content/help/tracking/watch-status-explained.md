Watch status is how WatchThis tracks your progress. It’s separate from lists, so you can reorganize your lists without losing your progress.

## Where to Set Watch Status

1. Open a title’s details view.
2. Use the watch status selector near the top.

Read: [View Details](/help/discover/view-details).

## Movie Statuses

Movies use a simple workflow:

- **Planning**: you want to watch it (but haven’t finished).
- **Completed**: you’ve watched it.

## TV Statuses

TV shows support more states:

- **Planning**: you plan to watch it.
- **Watching**: you’re actively watching.
- **Paused**: you’re taking a break.
- **Completed**: you’ve finished all currently available episodes.
- **Dropped**: you’ve stopped watching.

## How Status Interacts with Episodes and Schedules

### Episode tracking can update status

When you mark TV episodes watched, WatchThis may automatically move a show to **Watching**, and can mark it **Completed** when you finish the latest aired episode.

Read: [Track TV Episodes](/help/tracking/track-tv-episodes).

### Completed and dropped shows can’t be scheduled

Shows marked **Completed** or **Dropped** aren’t eligible for the weekly schedule. If you finish a show (or mark it dropped), it may disappear from scheduled prompts.

Read: [Weekly TV Schedule](/help/tracking/weekly-tv-schedule) and [Why did a Show Dissapear from My Schedule](/help/faq/why-did-a-show-dissapear-from-my-schedule).
