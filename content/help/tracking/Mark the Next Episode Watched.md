If you use the weekly schedule, WatchThis can prompt you on scheduled days and let you mark the “next” episode watched with one click.

## Where to Find the Prompt

You’ll see an **upcoming** card in your activity feed on days when:

- A show is scheduled for today, and
- You haven’t already watched an episode of that show today

[[Image: Activity feed card prompting “Have you watched this today?” with an “Episode Watched” button.]]

## Mark the Next Episode Watched

1. Open your activity feed on the dashboard.
2. Find the scheduled show card for today.
3. Click **Episode Watched**.

WatchThis saves your progress and updates your show status if needed.

## If the Button Says You’re Caught Up

This can happen when:

- There isn’t a next episode (you’ve finished what’s available), or
- The next episode hasn’t aired yet

In those cases, keep the show scheduled (so you’re reminded again later), or check the show’s episode list manually.

Read: [Track TV Episodes](/help/tracking/Track%20TV%20Episodes).
