For TV shows, WatchThis lets you mark individual episodes watched or unwatched so your progress is always accurate.

## Open Episode Tracking

1. Open a TV show’s details view.
2. Select the **Episodes** tab.

## Mark an Episode Watched (or Unwatched)

1. Find the season and episode you want.
2. Click the episode’s watched toggle.

Notes:

- Episodes that haven’t aired yet are disabled.
- When available, WatchThis shows the episode’s air date and the date you marked it watched.

## Mark a Whole Season

Each season includes quick actions:

- **Mark All**: marks all aired episodes in that season as watched.
- **Reset**: sets previously watched episodes in that season back to unwatched.

## How Episode Tracking Affects Watch Status

As you mark episodes watched, WatchThis can update your overall TV watch status automatically (for example moving a show to **Watching**, or marking it **Completed** when you catch up).

Read: [Watch Status Explained](/help/tracking/watch-status-explained).
